Izvještaj o transparentnosti ljudskog uticaja

Human Influence Transparency Report

Ovaj projekat funkcioniše po Ustavu (članovi 5 i 22) koji zahtijeva potpunu transparentnost. AI ne radi bez nadzora — ljudi intervenišu kada je potrebno, a svaka intervencija je javni zapis.

This project operates under a Constitution (Articles 5 and 22) that demands full transparency. The AI does not run unchecked — humans intervene when necessary, and every intervention is public record.

This page documents all human influence on the AI system: tasks directly filed by humans and instances where human operators overrode AI decisions in the government analysis pipeline or development workflow.

Kako zamjene funkcionišu

How Overrides Work

Administratori repozitorijuma mogu zamijeniti AI odluke na dva načina:

Repository administrators can override AI decisions in two ways:

  1. Ponovno otvaranje odbijenog pitanja: Ako AI trijaža odbije prijedlog, čovjek može ponovo otvoriti pitanje da ga premjesti u red čekanja za izvršenje.
  2. Dodavanje HUMAN OVERRIDE komentara: Svaki privilegovani korisnik može dodati komentar koji sadrži "HUMAN OVERRIDE" da premjesti pitanje direktno u red čekanja, zaobilazeći AI debatu.
  1. Reopen a rejected issue: If the AI triage rejects a proposal, a human can reopen the issue to move it to the backlog for execution.
  2. Add a HUMAN OVERRIDE comment: Any privileged user can add a comment containing "HUMAN OVERRIDE" to move an issue directly to the backlog, bypassing AI debate.

Svaka zamjena je zabilježena sa akterom, vremenskim pečatom, AI presudom, ljudskom akcijom i obrazloženjem. Ovo osigurava odgovornost i demonstrira da projekat funkcioniše transparentno — ne kao automatizacija bez nadzora, već kao AI analiza pod ljudskim nadzorom.

Every override is logged with the actor, timestamp, AI verdict, human action, and rationale. This ensures accountability and demonstrates that the project operates transparently — not as unchecked automation, but as human-supervised AI analysis.
